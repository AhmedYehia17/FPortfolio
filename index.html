About Me

Hi, I'm Fatima Elmehrath, a dedicated filmmaker and video editor with a passion for visual storytelling. With experience dating back to February 2023, I have successfully directed and edited a range of projects, including short films, documentaries, podcast episodes, and promotional content. My work highlights include directing and editing over four short films, crafting compelling film trailers, and executing a VFX advertisement project.

I am proficient in industry-standard software like Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve, Adobe After Effects, and Adobe Photoshop. My creativity and technical expertise allow me to bring unique and captivating stories to life.
